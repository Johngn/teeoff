A little bit about CHF Crumlin:

More than 150,000 children from all over Ireland attend Crumlin Hospital every year. CHF Crumlin exists to help save young lives, improve quality of life and support extraordinary care. CHF Crumlin's purpose is to fight the causes and effects of childhood illness, until all children can live healthy lives and reach their full potential. Over the past year CHF Crumlin has committed €8.4million to CHI at Crumlin and the National Children’s Research Centre. Specifically in the past year fundraising has supported:

  • €6.6m in grants to CHI, Crumlin & the NCRC.
  • €1m investment in cutting-edge equipment including operating Microscopes, Theatre Laparoscopic systems and equipment for the Genetics Lab.
  • €1m+ in 9 new Research programmes into immunology, infection, cancer and cardiology.
  • €300k invested in Ward renovations & upgrades across the hospital
  • 1000’s of lives saved through CHF Crumlin funded initiatives, research, equipment and access to education.
  • The 50k Giggle fund provided a multitude of experiences, outings and parties for children and families attending Crumlin along with an increase in the Music Therapy service to reach even more children attending the Hospital.
